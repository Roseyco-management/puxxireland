# PUXX Ireland - Visual Brand Guide

## Brand Adaptation for Irish Market

This guide outlines the visual identity for PUXX Ireland, adapting the global "World's Best Pouches" brand with Irish-inspired design elements while maintaining core brand recognition.

## Typography

### Primary Typeface: Montserrat (Bold, Geometric)
- **Usage:** Headlines, logo, product names
- **Weights:** Bold (700), ExtraBold (800), Black (900)
- **Character:** Modern, strong, geometric - matches PUXX's angular design

### Secondary Typeface: Inter
- **Usage:** Body copy, navigation, UI elements
- **Weights:** Regular (400), Medium (500), SemiBold (600)
- **Character:** Clean, readable, professional

### Typography Hierarchy

**H1 - Hero Headlines**
- Font: Montserrat ExtraBold
- Size: 72px / 4.5rem
- Color: Cream #F5E6D3 on green/black OR Deep Forest Green #00563F on white
- Letter-spacing: -1%

**H2 - Section Headers**
- Font: Montserrat Bold
- Size: 48px / 3rem
- Color: Emerald Green #009A49 OR Cream #F5E6D3
- Letter-spacing: -0.5%

**H3 - Subsections**
- Font: Montserrat Bold
- Size: 32px / 2rem
- Color: Deep Forest Green #00563F OR Shamrock Green #00A86B

**Body Copy**
- Font: Inter Regular
- Size: 16px / 1rem
- Line-height: 1.6
- Color: #333333 on light / #F5F5F5 on dark

**Captions / Small Text**
- Font: Inter Medium
- Size: 14px / 0.875rem
- Color: #666666 on light / #CCCCCC on dark

- Color: Same as logo or 70% opacity

## Do's and Don'ts

### DO:
✅ Use emerald green prominently throughout the site
✅ Maintain PUXX's bold, premium aesthetic
✅ Incorporate subtle Irish elements (shamrocks, Celtic patterns at low opacity)
✅ Use green gradients for energy and dynamism
✅ Keep typography bold and geometric
✅ Maintain black and gold for premium positioning
✅ Use Irish flag colors thoughtfully (green primary, white for space, orange sparingly)
✅ Adapt lightning effects to green
✅ Keep product color-coding consistent with global brand
✅ Use "18+" for Ireland (not 19+)

### DON'T:
❌ Overuse Irish clichés (leprechauns, rainbows, pots of gold)
❌ Make Celtic patterns too heavy or traditional
❌ Lose the PUXX core identity in Irish adaptation
❌ Use low-quality green (#00FF00 - avoid "toxic" green)
❌ Neglect accessibility (ensure green/text contrast meets WCAG standards)
❌ Use orange excessively (keep it as subtle accent only)
❌ Change product packaging colors drastically
❌ Make the site look like St. Patrick's Day year-round
❌ Use Comic Sans or decorative Irish fonts
❌ Compress or distort the PUXX logo

## Accessibility Standards

### Color Contrast
All text must meet WCAG AA standards:
- Normal text: 4.5:1 contrast ratio
- Large text (18pt+): 3:1 contrast ratio

**Tested Combinations:**
- Emerald Green #009A49 on White: ✅ 4.52:1 (AA)
- White on Emerald Green: ✅ 4.52:1 (AA)
- Deep Forest Green #00563F on White: ✅ 9.65:1 (AAA)
- Cream #F5E6D3 on Deep Forest Green: ✅ 8.12:1 (AAA)

### Interactive Elements
- All buttons have clear focus states
- Green outline for keyboard navigation
- Sufficient touch target size (44px minimum)
